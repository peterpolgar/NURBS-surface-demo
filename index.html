Usage: Hover mouse above the point you want to select, and on a selected point you can adjust the x, y, z coordinates and the weight of that point, all of these are feasible by scroll up / scroll down on the corresponding part of the appearing popup. Camera moving around the object: arrows, zoom in: key j, zoom out: key f.
